Meet Amanda

Hello from rural South Carolina! For the past 24 years my main roles have been mother, homeschooler, and homemaker. Once my four kids were a little older I started working part-time as a Adobe Lightroom instructor for Pretty Presets, and then later as a Quality Assurance analyst in the IT department of the company we homeschool with, Classical Conversations.

With my last child now a rising high school junior, I decided to get a jump start on my next adventure - web development! While it has been frustrating at times, I have thorougly enjoyed learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, the command line, GitHub, and Jest. The projects I am sharing are just a few of the many things we have learned. We have many more modules/months to go and I am excited about the new skills and concepts we will be tackling next.

You can find me at GitHub and LinkedIn as amandapadgett. The best way to contact me is via email.
