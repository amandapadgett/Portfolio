Meet Amanda

Hello from rural South Carolina! For the past 23 years my main roles have been mother, homeschooler, and homemaker. Once my four kids were a little older I started working part-time as a photographer and Adobe Lightroom instructor for Pretty Presets, and then later as a Qualtiy Assurance analyst for the company we homeschool through, Classical Conversations.

With my last child now a rising high school junior, I decided to get a jump start on my next adventure - web development! Thanks to my QA experience and all that I am learning via this bootcamp, I was recently offered the position of site administrator with the company I currently work for, where I will be using my new knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript (and also learning SQL).

You can find me at GitHub and LinkedIn as amandapadgett.
